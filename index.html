About

Hi, I'm Hannah, a web developer who I grew up on an organic farm outside of Cincinnati, OH. I now live in Philadelphia, PA.

I found my love for code while working for a farming organization, maintaining their social-media and helping write educational curriculum. Over time I became the go-to tech person, running webinars and maintaining their website. I started teaching myself HTML and CSS. I loved the feeling when I build my first website, being able to use logic and apply what I had learned to create something from scratch.

I decided to take the plunge and commit to learning web development full-time and from April 29th till June 19th 2015, I attended the Flatiron School, an intensive coding bootcamp in New York, NY. I had an amazing experience learning to be a full stack developer surrounded by a community equally enthusiastic about learning this new skill.

I love to be creative in everything I do and when I am not developing my latest side project, I can often be found cooking a meal from scratch, sawing a new clothing design, and gardening.
